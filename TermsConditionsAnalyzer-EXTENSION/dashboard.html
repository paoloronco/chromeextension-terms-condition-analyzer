About Legaly

Legaly is a powerful browser extension designed to make complex legal documents easy to understand. It uses advanced AI to analyze Terms and Conditions or Privacy Policies on any webpage, providing you with a clear and concise summary of the most important points.

How it Works

Simply click the ‘Analyze’ button in the popup, and the extension will scan the page content and securely send it to our backend for processing. In just seconds, you'll get a smart summary highlighting key topics like data collection, hidden fees, and your rights — so you can navigate the web with confidence.
